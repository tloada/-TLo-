El Pool

Inspirada por una famosa franquicia de video juegos, The Legend of ₳da está lista para presenciar la historia que esta siendo creada.
Bienvenidos a la revolución cripto.

  • Ecosistema Decentralizado

    Operando el pool de participación desde casa, combatiendo la centralización.

  • Pool Ecológico

    Operando en un Raspberry Pi reduce el consume de energía eléctrica a aproximadamente 5 Watts.

  • Technical Skills

    The Legend of ₳da participated on the different testnets leading to Shelley, getting experience as a stake pool operator.

  • Plug-N-Play

    The main advantage of delegation is that you can just delegate your ₳da and let it grow without worrying about it.

  • Passion

    The Legend of ₳da is determined on becoming one of the best Cardano stake pools. We love what we do.

  • Updates

    We are commited to be as transparent as possible with our delegators, frequently notifying them about the pool status and updates.
